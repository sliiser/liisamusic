Sky

Material Aspects

Sky is the most common element of our existence that we see every day in our lives. It is no surprise then that it s a very common dream symbol. To see sky in your dreams signifies your potential and also your mind. If you see yourself flying or floating in the sky, it could mean that you are trying to explore a new area of life but it could also mean that you are trying to break free from the ordinary. You are feeling sad and gloomy if the color of the sky in your dreams is dark. On the other hand, you are feeling happy if the sky is bright and sunny in your dreams.

Psychological/emotional perspective

Sky is a dream symbol that represents something that cannot be achieved. It tells you that your efforts will not suffice and that the thing you desire most will remain unattainable. This is the reason why people in ancient civilizations attached so much importance to their sky gods. If you find yourself looking up at the sky in your dreams, it is indicative of future success. It means there is someone in the higher echelons of the authority who will sow favor to you. This is an indication that the heaven is communicating with you and your stars will shine brightly in the near future. Thus you can consider yourself lucky if you see the skies in your dreams as it signifies future success for you.

Original Meaning

Sky is a very common dream symbol seen by millions of people in their dreams. To see clear skies in your dreams is symbolic of good times ahead. You can expect honors and achievements in your life if sky is a part of your dreams. However, sky also expresses your bad terms with the women in your life and also of broken promises. If you find yourself floating under the sky in the company of animals and other weird creatures, it is a sign of disbelief and bewilderment. It tells you that all the problem and worries in your life will translate into jealousy. This jealousy will somehow seep into your love life and your partner will see you with suspicion. Her loyalty will suffer and this would spell disaster for your relationship. Red sky in dreams is indicative of unrest among the public. You can expect violence and riots in the public when you see red skies in dreams.
